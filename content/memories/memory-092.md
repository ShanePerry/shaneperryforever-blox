I remember the night he arrived to Seattle Washington to propose to Vanessa. His flight came in so late that night that we had all gone to bed. The hotel was full from convention guests. With no other option, he found a way to make himself comfortable and fell asleep on the floor.

I also remember in 2004 we went to the Rawhide Theme Park in Arizona. We told the pretend Sheriff to arrest Shane when he came into the main street. They Sheriff came out, called his name and put him in handcuffs. There was a small trial and when they asked for his plea he said, "Guilty I guess." 

Some of Shane’s favorite things:

- Favorite dinner: Chicken Parmigiano
- Favorite breakfast: my bacon and eggs
- Favorite pastimes: reading, either a physical book or on a Kindle, sketching
- Favorite dream for the New World: building sailboats and transporting resurrected ones where they want to go
- Favorite Field Service snack in 2004: Gatorade and Doritos
- Favorite thing to say when asked to take a selfie for a group: My Mom gave me a selfie stick
