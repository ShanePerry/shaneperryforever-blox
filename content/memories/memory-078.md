We were visiting Bryan and Bethy Euler in Port Orchard, Washington. Shane had been in the backyard and he came in the house laughing. A little girl from the congregation was outside and he said that she scolded him for walking barefoot through the yard and inadvertantly stepping on a thorn patch. Shane hadn't even noticed, but said she looked up at him very seriously and said with disgust,  "You're stepping on a pokey."
