I never got to tell you how comforting Shane was when we came in September for the Gilead Grad and you sat in the same row close to us. We had just gone through some very stressful things and felt like we had no one who would understand or even care to.  But Shane immediately asked us what our challenges were and invited us to come to his office when we toured Warwick.  He really listened, he asked questions, even took us by the hand to speak with a brother who could help us.   I felt like Jehovah had answered our prayers through Shane.
