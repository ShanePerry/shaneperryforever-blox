Shane is one of the most warm and kind-hearted persons I've ever met.  Whenever we would run into each other, he would always say, "It's good to see you.  How have you been?" in a genuine way that I could tell he really cared.  I also remember talking to him recently and he told me a story about how he was dancing at a party and felt funny being a head taller than everyone else.  But I remember that because he shared an embarrassing story to make me feel better.  That's the Shane I know.  He always cared more about his friends.  And that's the Shane Jehovah knows, someone who was loving and showed consideration for the lowly ones. Psalm 46:1
